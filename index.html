Introduction

Welcome to Likhlo. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe experience while using our application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and handle your information when you use the Likhlo mobile application.

By downloading, installing, or using Likhlo, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • List Data: Names of shopping lists, item names, quantities, measurements, prices, and notes that you create within the App
  • Preferences: Your theme preference (dark mode/light mode), reminder settings, and notification preferences

1.2 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your phone number
  • We do NOT collect your email address
  • We do NOT collect your name or personal identity
  • We do NOT access your contacts, call logs, or messages
  • We do NOT access your camera, microphone, or photos
  • We do NOT access your location or GPS data
  • We do NOT track your browsing history
  • We do NOT collect any financial or payment information
  • We do NOT sell, rent, or trade any user data to third parties
  • We do NOT use any third-party analytics or tracking tools
  • We do NOT display any advertisements in the App

2. How Your Data is Stored

All your data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Your data stays on your phone/device
  • Your data is NOT uploaded to any server or cloud
  • Your data is NOT accessible to us or any third party
  • We have ZERO access to your lists, items, or any content you create

If you uninstall the App, all data will be permanently deleted. We recommend using the Share feature to back up important lists.

3. How We Use Information

  • Display your created lists and items within the App
  • Provide reminder/notification functionality
  • Save your theme preferences (dark/light mode)
  • Enable the Share feature

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do NOT share your data with any third parties
  • We do NOT sell your data to advertisers or data brokers

5. Data Security

  • All data is stored locally using standard secure storage mechanisms
  • The App does not transmit any data over the internet
  • Your data is protected by your device's own security (PIN, fingerprint, face lock)
  • We do not introduce any malware, spyware, or malicious code

6. Notifications and Permissions

Permissions We Request:

  • Notification Permission: To send reminder notifications (optional)
  • Storage Access: To save your data locally

Permissions We DO NOT Request:

7. Children's Privacy

Likhlo is suitable for all ages. We do NOT collect any personal information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal data at all, there is no risk of children's data being collected.

8. Third-Party Links

Likhlo does NOT contain any third-party links, advertisements, or embedded services. The App operates entirely offline.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the Last Updated date. Significant changes will be communicated through an in-app notification.

Your data is retained only on your device. You can delete your data by:
